Effective: July 13, 2026

LOOK5 is for direct, specific discussion of fashion looks. Critique the garment, styling, presentation, and design—not the person or body wearing it.

These Guidelines apply to reviews, tags, usernames, bios, reports, support messages, and other conduct connected to LOOK5.

## Prohibited content and conduct

Do not post or engage in:

- threats, encouragement of violence, or celebration of harm;
- harassment, bullying, stalking, or coordinated targeting;
- hateful or discriminatory attacks;
- sexual harassment, exploitation, or pornographic content;
- degrading comments about a model’s body, health, disability, identity, or personal traits;
- doxxing, private contact information, or instructions to locate a person;
- defamation, impersonation, or deceptive affiliation;
- spam, malware, irrelevant promotion, or repeated submissions;
- bots, scripts, deceptive accounts, or coordination intended to manipulate ratings, tags, reports, activity, or rankings;
- passwords, access tokens, financial details, government identifiers, private messages, or another person’s private information; or
- copyrighted text or other material you are not authorized to submit.

Tags must be short, relevant, one-word descriptions and may not contain slurs, harassment, sexual content, personal information, spam, or filter evasion.

## Reports

Use Report in good faith for another user’s eligible public review. A report creates a non-public evidence snapshot and does not automatically remove the original content. Do not submit knowingly false, retaliatory, or repetitive reports.

## Enforcement and appeals

LOOK5 may reject, hide, or remove content; dismiss or resolve reports; restrict a feature; suspend or terminate an account; preserve evidence; and contact authorities when appropriate.

Public removal does not require deletion of report snapshots or other audit evidence. Enforcement may consider severity, context, history, intent, and risk.

LOOK5 targets review of ordinary reports within 48 hours but does not guarantee a resolution time. Urgent safety and security matters receive priority.

Appeals may be sent to [look5founder@gmail.com](mailto:look5founder@gmail.com) with the username, affected look/content, action, and reason for appeal.

For immediate danger, contact local emergency services first. LOOK5 support is not an emergency service.
