Effective: July 13, 2026

For app issues, account help, privacy requests, moderation concerns, broken source links, security concerns, copyright matters, or general feedback, email:

[look5founder@gmail.com](mailto:look5founder@gmail.com)

Include your LOOK5 username if relevant, the affected look or collection, the app version/build, what happened, and a safe screenshot if it helps reproduce the issue. Never send a password, access token, or unnecessary sensitive information.

LOOK5 targets review of ordinary reports and support matters within 48 hours. Urgent safety or security matters are prioritized for same-day handling. These are targets, not guaranteed resolution times or 24/7 coverage.

For immediate danger or suspected criminal activity, contact local emergency services. LOOK5 support is not an emergency service.

## Report a public review

Use Report beside another user’s eligible public review. A report does not automatically remove content; it creates a non-public moderation record.

## Delete an account

Open Profile → Settings → Delete account. Successful deletion is permanent and signs you out. Contact Support if the in-app flow fails.

## Broken or inappropriate external source

Send the look or collection identifier, source label, destination URL, and the issue. LOOK5 can correct or disable its link but cannot remove content hosted by an external service.

## Privacy request

State the right or request you want to exercise and the LOOK5 account involved. LOOK5 may need to verify your identity before disclosing or changing account-linked information.

## Copyright

See the [Copyright and Takedown process](/copyright/).
